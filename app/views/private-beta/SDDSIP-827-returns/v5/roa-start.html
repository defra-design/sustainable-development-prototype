Send a report of action taken under badger mitigation licence 2021-09273-SPM-WLM

Use this service to report on action taken under a licence to interfere with badger setts as part of development work.

You must submit a report of action taken under the licence within 14 working days of a licence expiring. You must submit a report of action even if you did not do any of the activities you were licensed to carry out.

Find out about reporting your compliance with a wildlife licence.

Before you start

You will need:

  • details of each licensed activity taken at each badger sett
  • details of any artificial setts created
  • an understanding of any potential breaches of the licence
  • any concerns about badgers' welfare as a result of action taken under the licence
  • any files that will support the evidence you submit in your report

If you need to submit a report of action taken under this licence but are unable to use digital services, email [assisted_digital_email].
