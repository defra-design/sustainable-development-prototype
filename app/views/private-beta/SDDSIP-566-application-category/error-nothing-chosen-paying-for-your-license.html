Paying for your wildlife licence

You may not have to pay for a wildlife licence if your development is on certain kinds of sites, including:

  • registered places of worship
  • scheduled monuments
  • listed buildings
  • traditional farm buildings in a countryside stewardship agreement
  • homes being improved through planning permission or permitted development
