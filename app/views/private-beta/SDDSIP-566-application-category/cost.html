Cost of this licence

The cost of this licence ranges from £171 to £768.

The cost varies depending on how complex your application is. Things that affect this include:

  • the number of setts affected
  • whether a protected site is affected
  • whether badgers will be present
  • likely impact on badgers
  • methods used
  • completeness and conciseness of the information in the application

The cost may be more if you need to make a change to your application once you have submitted it.
