Purpose of the development work

To register a site for bat mitigation work, you must show that the reason for the development on the site either:

  • has no satisfactory alternative
  • is to preserve public health and safety
  • is for overriding public interest

In most cases, you will need to submit a reasoned statement (opens in new tab).
